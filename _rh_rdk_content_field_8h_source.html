10 /** \class CRhRdkContentField
11 
12  RDK content fields are essentially smart variants which have an internal name and local name
13  and English name along with the usual variant data. By using fields to store your content's
14  data items instead of using plain bools, floats and so on, you get automatic parameter undo
15  support, support for transferring to and from IRhRdk_XMLSection and a few other features that
16  should reduce the amount of work involved in implementing your content.
17 
18  If you want to use fields to store your content's data items, you must make sure it overrides
19  BitFlags() and sets the bfFields flag.
20 
21  \see CRhRdkContent.
22  \see CRhRdkContentFields.
23  \see CRhRdkContent::BitFlags().
