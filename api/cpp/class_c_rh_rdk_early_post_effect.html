Detailed Description

3rd-party early post effects should subclass this class.

Early post effects must expect the channel data to possibly be greater than 1 (i.e., high dynamic range) and they must preserve the high dynamic range (HDR); it is the job of the following tone mapping stage to convert HDR to LDR.
